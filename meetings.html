Club Meetings

Club meetings are held at the Dupage Airport Flight Center and begin at 7:30 PM. The West Chicago airport (KDPA) is 31 miles west of downtown Chicago and is located between North Avenue and Roosevelt Rd., about 4 miles west of Route 59.

Board meetings are held on the first Thursday of the month and the general membership meetings are held on the second Thursday of the month.

In case of a picnic, plane wash or party, the membership meeting will be moved - we inform members via email and meeting minutes, so if you are a guest or prospective member, Ask Us about the next meeting location.
